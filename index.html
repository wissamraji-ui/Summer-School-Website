Modular Forms and Related Topics in Number Theory

A three-day intensive program connecting students and researchers through mini-courses, research discussions, and regional collaboration.

May 17-19, 2026 Akdeniz University, Antalya, Turkey Organized with NTRU at CAMS, AUB
View Schedule Meet Speakers Register

Organized by the Number Theory Research Unit at the Center for Advanced Mathematical Sciences (CAMS), AUB.

Program Snapshot

  • 4 mini-courses by international experts
  • 3 full days of lectures and interaction
  • Graduate-level focus on modular forms and L-series
  • Breakout sessions for direct student-faculty exchange

About The School

Building a Regional Hub for Number Theory

Vision

The school fosters high-level collaboration between Turkish and Lebanese mathematical communities through a focused program on modular forms and neighboring areas in number theory.

Objectives

  • Strengthen regional research networks
  • Transfer advanced knowledge to graduate students
  • Spark joint projects and future publications
